I have converted some functionality of my programs, implemented new functions or maintained packages by colleagues into R. as reported in gap: Genetic Analysis Package. Most are on the Rockefeller list of genetic software. The examples provided for SAS, Stata and R can be considered as extension to those in Genetic Dissection of Complex Traits In Silico: Approaches, Problems and Solutions. A summary of my recent work on mixed models is described in Mixed Modeling with Whole Genome Data and Bayesian Linear Mixed Models with Polygenic Effects.
